Puzzle Pazza

Are you bored? Try Puzzle Pazza!

You can choose three puzzles of different levels. Each level consists from 3 sizes of the puzzle (3x3, 4x4 and 5x5).

The game is started by the green button, stopped by the red one.

You move the pieces by clicking on them.

The empty field is always the bottom right one.

Try to solve the puzzle as fast as you can to get the best score.
